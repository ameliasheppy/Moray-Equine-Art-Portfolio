Model Horse Info

What is a model horse?

Many model horse companies around the world bring the joy of "owning" a horse to avid kids and adults. Some may have yearned for a horse for their entire lives, and just this small model makes them feel like they are part of something bigger. Have you ever seen a majestic horse and thought "I'd like one of those!" Do you own, or have a permanent home taken in your heart for a beloved horse? You can buy various original finish models that come done in realistic or fantasy colors. But a long time ago, someone very creative (I'm not sure who) had the idea to sculpt and paint onto these factory produced model horses and make them special, custom works of heart, er I mean art.

Where to buy model horses

Various companies sell model horses at different price ranges. Breyer Horses is a great company to start with. They offer several scales of models at prices you'll feel comfortable letting your kids play on the floor with, mid-level price models, and on up to "Leave that mortgage-payment of a test run model in a glass cabinet!" Another fairly popular model horse company, mostly among collectors, not kids, is The Peter Stone Company. Stone horses are more pricey than Breyers, but arguably more realistic in several ways.
