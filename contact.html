Their social media information will be linked with their work, go check them out and see more of their hard work.

DeeJayBe Studios by Darynn Bednarczyk

DeeJayBe Studios Website

Ms Bednarczyk makes some truly jaw-dropping art! She's an award winning artist that has a keen eye for details that many wouldn't even notice. She uses Breyers, Peter Stone Horses, resin models by other artists, and resins by her own design to make incredibly detailed and realistic models. The eyelashes on some of them will make you think her horses are absolutely going to blink!
